About Checkout Frenzy

Have you ever stood in line at a store and wondered, "What exactly are the cashiers doing on that screen?" All those bippity boppity beeps and flashing buttons... What do they even mean? Now, you can experience it yourself!

Checkout Frenzy takes that cashier job and turns it into a super fun online game. You get to be the one behind the register, running your own mini-mart from scratch. You're stocking chips, checking out customers, and hiring helpers - everything's in your hands.

This online game lets you experience the chaos and challenge of being a cashier and a store manager at the same time. From scanning groceries to calculating change, from setting prices to placing shelves. It's all about building the busiest store on the block!

Game Controls

  • WASD: Move around the store
  • Drag left click: Look around
  • Left Click: Pick up boxes, scan items, place goods, rotate furniture, confirm purchases

Starting Your Store

When you first enter the game, it's just you, a register, a computer and an empty space. Walk up to the computer in the corner of the store and use it to order furniture like shelves, fridges, or freezers. Don't forget to order products too - things like chips, soda, ice cream, and milk. Boxes will appear outside your store in a few seconds. Go grab them and bring them in.

Stocking Your Shelves

Time to get your goods in place! Dry goods (chips, candy) go on regular shelves. Cold items (milk, eggs) go in the fridge. Frozen foods (ice cream, frozen pizza) belong in the freezer. Fruits and vegetables go in the grocery shelves. You can rotate shelves to create a better store layout with space for customers to walk around.

Setting Prices

Every product has a base price, but you decide what to charge. Hover on the price tag and click the set price button. Adjust your prices carefully - raise them to earn more, but don't go too high or customers will walk out!

Serving Customers

When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign from "Closed" to "Open". Customers will start rolling in with their shopping carts! Click on each item to scan it, listen for that classic "beep". Check how they want to pay and click on the card or cash they're holding. For card payments, punch in the total amount correctly. For cash, choose the correct bills and coins to give the right change.

Growing Your Business

Once your store is booming, hire cashiers to handle checkouts or cleaners to keep the place tidy. If you have extra cash, buy more floor space to fit more shelves, attract more customers, and make more money!

How to Play

Scan Products

Quickly scan items by dragging them across the scanner. Accuracy matters - don't miss any barcodes!

Beat the Clock

Serve customers as quickly as possible. The faster you work, the more points you'll earn!

Level Up

Progress through increasingly challenging levels with more demanding customers and complex orders.

Tips & Tricks for Success

  • Strategic Store Layout: Create an efficient layout with space for customers to move around. Place popular items near the entrance to attract customers.

  • Smart Pricing: Find the sweet spot for pricing - set prices slightly above market value but not so high that customers leave. Different products can have different markup percentages.

  • Fast Checkout: Group similar items when scanning, memorize produce codes, and have the right change ready. Speed matters in keeping customers happy!

  • Timely Hiring: Hire staff when your store gets busy - but remember they're only hired for one day. Make sure your profits justify the extra help.

  • Product Variety: Stock a wide range of products to appeal to different customer preferences and maximize your sales potential.
