About Checkout Frenzy

Checkout Frenzy puts you in the shoes of a supermarket cashier during peak shopping hours. Your goal is simple: serve as many customers as possible while maintaining accuracy and speed.

As the queues grow longer and customers become more impatient, you'll need to scan items quickly, handle payments correctly, and provide excellent customer service to earn high scores and progress through increasingly challenging levels.

With realistic cashier mechanics, diverse customer personalities, and unexpected events like price checks and coupon validations, Checkout Frenzy delivers an authentic and addictive retail experience that will test your multitasking abilities!

How to Play

Scan Items

Click or tap on items to scan them as they come down the conveyor belt. Be careful not to miss any products!

Process Payments

Handle different payment methods including cash, credit cards, and mobile payments. Make sure to count change correctly!

Manage Time

Keep an eye on the queue and try to serve customers efficiently. The faster you work, the higher your score will be!

Game Features

Multiple Challenge Levels

Progress through increasingly difficult scenarios, from quiet morning shifts to chaotic holiday shopping rushes.

Realistic Simulation

Experience authentic cashier challenges including product lookups, price checks, and handling difficult customers.

Score Tracking

Compete with friends and other players to climb the global leaderboard and become the ultimate checkout champion.

Skill Progression

Unlock special abilities and upgrades as you improve your cashier skills and earn experience points.
