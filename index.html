About The Game

Enter the high-pressure world of retail checkout where every second counts!

Checkout Frenzy puts you in the shoes of a supermarket cashier during the busiest shopping hours. Your mission? Scan items, calculate totals, give correct change, and keep the customers happy as you face increasingly challenging waves of shoppers.

With realistic cashier mechanics, time pressure, and a variety of customer interactions, Checkout Frenzy offers an authentic and addictive gameplay experience that tests your speed, accuracy, and multitasking abilities.

Whether you're looking for a quick casual game or want to master the art of checkout efficiency, Checkout Frenzy provides hours of entertainment with its simple-to-learn but hard-to-master gameplay. Can you handle the pressure when the lines get long and customers get impatient?

How To Play

Master these steps to become a checkout champion!

1

Scan Items

Click or tap on items to scan them. Watch for barcodes and make sure they register correctly.

2

Apply Discounts

Process coupons and special offers by entering their codes or scanning them when presented.

3

Calculate Totals

Finalize the transaction by calculating the total amount and tax where applicable.

4

Process Payment

Accept cash or card payments and provide the correct change to cash-paying customers.

5

Keep Customers Happy

Monitor customer satisfaction and aim for positive feedback by being quick and accurate.

6

Level Up

Complete levels to unlock new stores, items, and challenges with increasing difficulty.
